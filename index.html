Intuit QuickNav
intuit.com screenshot

Replacing what was previously bulky and overly-complex, I developed a new header used across Intuit.com. With speed a high priority, I worked closely with the Intuit design team to move away from cross-browser "pixel perfection". Instead we took advantage of CSS3 to enhance the visual design, while making sure older browsers still enjoyed a completely usable experience; a first for Intuit.

Keywords:
HTML/CSS/JS, CSS3, jQuery
Website

Intuit Performance Overhaul
webpagetest.org screenshot of intuit.com results

As part of a small consulting team I performed a performance overhaul on Intuit.com - ranked in the US Top 100 by Alexa - as well as Intuit's 50 other most frequented pages. We accomplished a halving of page load times, driving a marked increase in growth.

HTML/CSS/JS, performance analysis, intense refactoring, build automation, accessibility enhancement
